## Starting Out

1. Login to your dashboard from `https://franciscopina.com`
2. Click on "Create New Project"
3. Name your documentation project
4. Begin adding sections to organize topics

## Tips

- Use clear section titles
- Drag-and-drop to reorder
- Save regularly to avoid data loss
