## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for registration
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Optional: Your project files ready for upload (Markdown, images, or PDFs up to 10MB each)

</Callout>

Review these to avoid delays during setup.

## Create Your Account

Sign up quickly to access your documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://franciscopinacomendador.com/app`.

    Click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register" icon="user-plus">
    Enter your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.

    Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and click **Log In**.

    You'll land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Organize your projects by creating a dedicated space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space** or the `+` icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Name it (e.g., "My Project Docs"), add a description like "Central hub for project notes", and select visibility: public, private, or team-shared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create**. You'll enter the space editor with a blank page ready for content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your Initial Project Files

Add your project files to populate the space.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="upload">
    Open your space editor.

    Drag files from your desktop directly into the file panel on the left sidebar.

    Supported formats include `.md`, `.png`, `.pdf`. Files appear instantly with previews.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Upload" icon="code">
    Use the upload API for automation.

    <CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
      ````bash
      curl -X POST https://franciscopinacomendador.com/api/v1/spaces/YOUR_SPACE_ID/files \
        -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
        -F "file=@/path/to/project.md"
      ````

      ````javascript
      const formData = new FormData();
      formData.append('file', fileInput.files[0]);

      fetch('https://franciscopinacomendador.com/api/v1/spaces/YOUR_SPACE_ID/files', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: { 'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_TOKEN' },
        body: formData
      });
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<ParamField path="space_id" param-type="string" required="true">
  Unique identifier for your documentation space (found in space settings).
</ParamField>

<ParamField header="Authorization" param-type="string" required="true">
  Bearer token from your account settings.
</ParamField>
